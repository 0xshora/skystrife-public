Due to the Orb emission schedule, players finishing in third or fourth place often experience a net loss in Orbs, making it challenging for newcomers and beginners to casually engage in matches. To address this, we have developed a system to objectively assess player skills, laying the groundwork for future improvements in matchmaking.
